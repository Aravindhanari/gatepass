Apartment security and visitors Management

Security management systems in apartments and gated community have always been based on primitive pen and paper system. Security guards spend most of their time in communicating visitor details with the residents. Even after all this there is no visibility into who is inside the community and who is outside.There is so much information about apartment service staff at the main gate, yet residents have anxiety every morning about their household help. myGate solves all these security and visitor problems with an intelligent app based solution.

Apartments can deploy myGate security solution in less than a week without any investment in hardware or any capital expenditure. We give you the security device in a pay as you go pricing model. When we deploy myGate Apartment Security and Visitor Management System, we take care of the training of community management committee, facility staff and security guards. All this at a negligible cost. myGate Security management system is free from any costly hardware installation. myGate is installed in all the popular and premium properties in Bangalore including Mantri Espana, Adarsh Palm Retreat, Purva Riviera, Prestige Ozone etc.

This security management system for Apartments and houses can be deployed in a multi-tiered system where there are multiple security check points, viz. At the main gate and at the building gate. This software also helps you manage visitor car parking easily. Finding out the owner of a randomly parked vehicle inside the community is always a hassle. myGate app will connect you directly with the owner of the vehicle in such cases. The app is easy to use and some necessary functions of this app can be used even if the residents do not have a mobile or a smartphone. The security system is designed in a way that is equally hassle free for senior citizens and children
